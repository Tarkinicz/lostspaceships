RETRO SPACE SHOOTER


You play the role of a pilot who wants to save the universe. For this purpose, he embarks on a journey to find spaceships of pilots who failed their mission to tip the balance in favour of his allies.


Unlock new spaceships, upgrade your machine, face up to the hordes of various, cosmic invaders and defeat bosses.
